Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the Sleep Lounge?

You only need to register once by completing our online registration and waiver form. After registration, you can visit either campus location during operating hours and staff will check you in.

Is the Zen Zone available at both campuses?

No, the Zen Zone is currently only available at the Lakeshore Campus in the basement of the K Building.

How long can I use the Sleep Lounge?

Sleep Lounge sessions are limited to one hour per student per day. This ensures that we can accommodate as many students as possible.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just your student ID! We provide clean bedding and a personal eye mask for your use. For hygiene reasons, no outside bedding is permitted.

Ready to Experience Our Services?

Register once and access both the Sleep Lounge and Zen Zone services.
