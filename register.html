Sleep Lounge & Zen Zone Guidelines

We want your experience in the IGNITE services to be the best possible. Please note the following guidelines to ensure privacy, safety, and a positive experience for all students:

Required for Entry
  • Present your student card (no other ID accepted)
  • Complete this registration form once
Quiet Environment
  • No talking in quiet zones
  • Devices on silent mode
  • Use headphones at low volume
  • Set vibrating alarms only
Not Permitted
  • No food or drinks
  • No more than one person per bed/station
  • No flash photography
  • No aromatherapy devices or sprays
  • No outside bedding (for hygiene reasons)
Important Notes
  • 60-minute time limit per session
  • One session per student per day
  • Staff may tap you if you don't wake up
  • Wipe down stations after use
  • IGNITE is not responsible for lost/stolen items
Agreement and Liability Waiver

By checking the box below, I acknowledge that:

  • I have read and agree to follow all guidelines.
  • I understand that using these services is entirely at my own risk.
  • I release IGNITE Student Life from any liability for any injury or loss that may occur during my use of their services.
  • I consent to my registration information being stored for verification purposes.
