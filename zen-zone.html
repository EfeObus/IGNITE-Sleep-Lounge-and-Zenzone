Welcome to the Zen Zone

Explore Features

Find Your Peace

The IGNITE Zen Zone is the perfect place for students to relax, hang out, and study. It's where you can go to tune out the noise and tune in to your thoughts. The Zen Zone can be your go-to spot in between classes, a safe space to practice mindfulness, or your trusted retreat for all the studying you need.

"In the midst of movement and chaos, keep stillness inside of you."
- Deepak Chopra

What We Offer

Our Space

Zen Zone is more than a quiet space—it redefines relaxation! Start your day at the gratitude wall, sip free tea, or sink into ultra-comfy bean bag chairs. Unwind with coloring books or find peace in the meditation areas. Every corner of this room is designed to help you recharge and embrace mindfulness.

  • Free tea
  • Comfortable seating
  • Coloring books
  • Meditation areas

Happy Painter

In collaboration with the Humber Spirituality and Wellness Centre, grab a brush and let the creativity flow at the IGNITE Zen Zone painting sessions. With limitless imagination in the air, these events are the perfect way to relax and express yourself through art.

Pop-Up Library

IGNITE Zen Zone brings students closer to their next great read. In partnership with Humber Library, Humber Idea Lab, and Humber Archives, our Pop-Up Library events transform the Zen Zone into a literary escape with books from every genre. Combined with themed photobooths and card making, there's something for everyone.
