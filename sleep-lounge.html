Recharge Between Classes

The IGNITE Sleep Lounge is designed to provide students with a safe, comfortable environment to take short power naps between classes. Studies have shown that short naps can significantly improve alertness, mood, and academic performance. Our specially designed sleeping pods offer privacy and comfort when you need it most.

"Sleep is the golden chain that binds health and our bodies together."
- Thomas Dekker

How The Sleep Lounge Works

Our Sleep Pods

Each Sleep Lounge pod is designed to provide a comfortable, private space for power napping. The pods are equipped with clean bedding, personal alarms, and complimentary eye masks to help you get the best rest possible.

  • 60-minute time slots
  • Safe & secure environment
  • Comfortable sleeping surface
  • Complimentary eye masks

How To Use It

Using the Sleep Lounge is simple! First, complete a one-time online registration and waiver form. Then, visit any Sleep Lounge location during operating hours and present your student ID to the staff on duty. Staff will check you in and assign you to an available pod.

  • Register once online
  • Show your student ID
  • Enjoy a 60-minute rest
  • Available daily

Benefits of Power Napping

Scientific research has shown that short naps (20-60 minutes) can provide significant benefits for students, including improved memory retention, enhanced cognitive function, better mood, and reduced stress levels. The Sleep Lounge gives you access to an environment optimized for these short, effective rest periods.

  • Improved cognitive function
  • Enhanced memory retention
  • Better mood and alertness
  • Reduced stress levels

Sleep Lounge Guidelines

For Your Use

  • Present your student card to use the pods
  • Set a vibrating alarm on your phone
  • Use headphones for music at a low volume
  • Wipe down the pod after use with provided materials

Not Permitted

  • No food or drinks in the Sleep Lounge
  • No talking (it's a quiet zone)
  • No more than one person per pod
  • No flash photography
  • No aromatherapy devices or sprays

Please Note

  • Maximum 60 minutes per session
  • Limited to once per day per student
  • Staff may tap your shoulder when time is up
  • IGNITE is not responsible for lost/stolen items
